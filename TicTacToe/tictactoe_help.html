🎯 Basic Game Rules

Objective

Be the first player to get three of your symbols (X or O) in a row - horizontally, vertically, or diagonally on the 3×3 grid.

X
O
O
X
X

Example: X wins with a diagonal line!

How to Play

  1. Player 1 (X) always goes first
  2. Click on any empty cell to place your symbol
  3. Players alternate turns
  4. The game ends when someone gets three in a row or the board is full (draw)
  5. Start a new game by clicking "Reset"

🎮 Game Modes & Controls

Game Mode Selection

  • vs AI Player: Play against the computer with adjustable difficulty
  • vs Player 2: Two human players take turns on the same device

Difficulty Levels (AI Mode Only)

  • Easy: AI makes random moves with basic blocking
  • Medium: AI uses some strategy and occasionally takes center/corners
  • Hard: AI uses advanced strategy, prioritizes center and corners, and uses power-ups intelligently

Each player starts with 3 random power-ups per game. Use them wisely - each can only be used once!

🎯 Double Strike
Place two symbols in one turn (they cannot immediately form a winning line)
Strategy: Best used early in the game when you have more placement options. Great for controlling multiple areas of the board.
🔄 Convert
Convert one of your opponent's symbols to yours
Strategy: Extremely powerful for stealing wins or disrupting opponent's plans. Cannot affect shielded symbols.
🛡️ Shield
Protect one of your symbols from being converted or affected by other power-ups
Strategy: Use on symbols that are part of your winning strategy or in strategic positions like the center.
🚫 Block
Prevent your opponent from placing a symbol in a specific cell
Strategy: Perfect for blocking opponent's winning moves or controlling key positions like corners and center.
🔄 Rotate Board
Spin the entire board 90 degrees clockwise
Strategy: Can completely change the game dynamics. Use when you're behind or to disrupt opponent's winning patterns.
⏪ Rewind
Undo the last move made by either player
Strategy: Save this for when your opponent makes a great move or you make a mistake. Very powerful in critical moments.
🔗 Steal
Take a random unused power-up from your opponent
Strategy: Best used early when opponent has many power-ups left. Can turn the tide by gaining extra abilities.
💣 Bomb
Clear the center area and place your symbol there
Strategy: Center control is crucial in Tic Tac Toe. Use when center is occupied by opponent or blocked.

🧠 Strategy & Tips

Basic Strategy (No Power-ups)

  • Control the Center: The middle cell is part of 4 possible winning lines
  • Take Corners: Corner cells are part of 3 possible winning lines each
  • Block Opponents: Always block when opponent has 2 in a row
  • Create Forks: Try to create multiple winning threats simultaneously

Power-up Strategy

  • Save Defensive Power-ups: Keep Shield, Block, and Rewind for critical moments
  • Use Offensive Early: Double Strike and Convert are more effective with more options
  • Time Rotate Carefully: Can be devastating but also risky - use when you understand the new layout
  • Shield Strategically: Protect center cell or cells that complete multiple winning lines
  • Block Key Positions: Block opponent's center or corner rather than random cells

Advanced Tips

  • Power-up Timing: Don't use all power-ups early - save at least one for endgame
  • Mind Games: In 2-player mode, sometimes showing you have a powerful power-up can influence opponent's moves
  • AI Adaptation: On Hard difficulty, the AI uses power-ups strategically based on game state
  • Combo Planning: Some power-ups work well together (Shield + Convert protection)

🖥️ Interface Guide

Main Game Area

  • Game Board: 3×3 grid where you click to place symbols
  • Status Display: Shows whose turn it is and game messages
  • Score Tracker: Displays wins, losses, and draws for the current session

Power-up Controls (When Enabled)

  • Player Power-up Lists: Shows available power-ups with descriptions
  • Use Power-up Buttons: Click to activate selected power-up
  • Power-up Status: Displays messages about power-up effects
  • [USED] Tags: Mark power-ups that have already been consumed

Game Settings

  • Game Mode Dropdown: Switch between AI and human opponent
  • Difficulty Selector: Choose AI difficulty (only active in AI mode)
  • Enable Power-ups Checkbox: Toggle advanced gameplay mode

🔧 Troubleshooting

Common Issues

Power-up Not Working

  • Make sure power-ups are enabled
  • Check that it's your turn (in AI mode, only use power-ups on your turn)
  • Verify the power-up hasn't been used already ([USED] tag)
  • Some power-ups require valid targets (symbols to swap/shield, cells to block)

Can't Click on Cell

  • Cell might be blocked (red background)
  • Cell might already contain a symbol
  • Game might be over - click "Reset" to restart
  • If Double Strike is active, you need to place your second symbol

AI Not Moving

  • Make sure you're in AI mode, not 2-player mode
  • Wait a moment - AI takes time to think, especially on Hard difficulty
  • If AI is using a power-up, there's a 1-second delay for visual effect

Game Features

  • Hover Effects: Gray highlighting shows where you can place symbols
  • Winning Animation: Winning line flashes and highlights at game end
  • Symbol Animation: Symbols fade in when placed for visual appeal
  • Smart AI: On Hard difficulty, AI analyzes threats and uses power-ups strategically
