*** Commands: *** (current)
CharTrek-TFO (The First One)

Main Menu >>>>

Start!    - Start playing the game.
Help      - Displays this help text.
Options   - Change difficulty, your name, register, etc.
About     - A little info about the game.
Exit      - Exit the game.

In Game >>>>

SubLight - Move starship (sub-light) to Quadrant x,y.
JmpDrive - Move starship (jump) to Sector x,y.

Shields   - Raises and lowers ships deflector shields.
Transport - Activate transporter to transport to planet.

Lasers   - Fire main lasers. They can be fired 360 degrees, max range of 3.
Torpedos - Launch torpedo. They can also be fired 360 degrees, max range of 5.

Engineer - Make emergency repairs on shields, hull, or engines.
Docking  - Allows you to dock with a starbase for repairs.

SRScan - Short Range Scan - used to scan for cloaked enemies or other
         objects within the current sector.
LRScan - Long Range Scan - used to check for enemy activity in nearby
         sectors. (max range is 3, 360 degrees)

Tractor  - Use this to hold enemies in place while attacking.
Legend   - Shows the object legend.

MainMenu - Return to the main menu.
Exit     - Exit the game now.

More >>>>

Heading Display - sets the direction of the ship. Also sets direction
for firing weapons.

Move Display - set distance for sub-light or jump drive (1-9).

Range Display - set the range for lasers (1-3) or torpedoes (1-5).

You can also click the 'X' button to cancel commands.

*** Options: ***

Difficulty - Use this to set the number of enemies, power, health,
damage...etc. Three settings, Easy, Medium, and Hard.

Name - Change your name.

Sound - Changes sound settings to either On or Off.

Music/MP3 - Music setting lets you select a track from the MP3 list and
set whether to play it once or to loop it (repeat). NOTE: all MP3's
must be copied to the 'mp3s' folder. (max of 26)

Register - Let's you register the game to `ove the time limit and
allow you to access the mp3 options. (requires an unlock code)

<- Back - Returns you to the game and saves your current settings.

*** Hints and Tips: ***

Everything on your ship uses 'energy' so take care on how much is
used at all times. If you find yourself low on energy, locate the
nearest planet to mine energy crystals or dock at a nearby starbase
for repairs if available.

When using sublight or jump drive use caution when going over distances
of 0.7 (sublight) or 7.0 (jumpdrive), this may cause engine overheating
therefore causing engine damage.

If entering a sector that has enemies, make sure you use your shields
to absorb the enemy fire if hit. if shields are down you WILL take
hull damage.

You have crew you need to keep an eye on. Lose too many crew members
and your ship will start to lose it's efficiency and you will suffer
more damage than usual. You can replenish crew ONLY at a starbase.

If you are 500 or above on energy, going to a planet to transport for
mining crystals will be of no use, so don't bother.

When making emergency repairs, any system that is below 50% (i.e.
shields, hull, or engines) will be considered repairable.

When docking with a starbase,transporting to a planet, or entering
a stable wormhole you move directly ON the object and press the.
appropriate button. (if applicable)

When carrying out a long range scan, enemies can still move from
sector to sector....even into YOUR current sector!
